Cindy: The Community Co-Pilot Project Report

Project Overview

Cindy, the community co-pilot, is an AI-powered suite of tools designed to accelerate the go-to-market of key projects and founders within the Stacks ecosystem. This project is funded by the Stacks Grants organization with a budget of $1,300.

Project Goals

  • Accelerate the go-to-market of key projects and founders in the Stacks ecosystem using AI to accelerate recruiting, onboarding, ideation, prototyping, and product development.
  • Level up the awareness and capabilities of ecosystem participants to use AI to accelerate their own growth.

Key Components

  • Interactive Stacks Ecosystem Visualization for Rapid Onboarding (embeddable)
  • AI-powered chatbot with multi-agent capabilities (FAQ Agent, Recruiting, Onboarding, & Cross-Pollination of Talent) (embeddable)
  • Stacks AI Buildathons to show the ecosystem how to harness the power of AI and stacks (3+)
